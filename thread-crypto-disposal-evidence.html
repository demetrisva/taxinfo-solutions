Evidence framework

Feb 16, 2026

When sharing examples, identify disposal type (sale, swap, conversion) and include timestamps, platform export, and valuation basis at the event time.

  • Keep wallet addresses mapped to owner entity.
  • Archive trade exports in immutable format.
  • Store FX conversion basis used for reports.

Expert reminder

Feb 14, 2026

Evidence quality matters more than spreadsheet complexity. Keep the chain of records clear from source trade to final tax computation file.

Records to Keep

  • Exchange exports by period.
  • Wallet movement log.
  • Transaction hash index.
  • Valuation and FX snapshot notes.
