Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

1. Calculator Inputs Stay on Your Device

We do not collect, store, or transmit the financial figures you type into calculators.

When you use our "Salary Calculator" or "Business Profit Calculator":

  • All calculations happen locally on your device (Client-Side JavaScript).
  • Your salary, profit, or tax inputs are never sent to our servers.
  • Once you close the browser tab, your input data vanishes.
