Common Questions on 2026 Reforms

What is the tax-free salary in Cyprus for 2026?
The tax-free threshold has increased to ‚Ç¨22,000. Income above this is taxed at progressive rates starting at 20%.

How does the 15% Corporate Tax affect dividends?
While the corporate tax rate rose to 15%, the Special Defence Contribution (SDC) on dividends for tax residents was reduced to 5%, often lowering the total effective tax rate.

Is there a tax on Cryptocurrency in Cyprus?
Yes, effective 2026, a flat tax of 8% applies to profits from the disposal of crypto-assets.
