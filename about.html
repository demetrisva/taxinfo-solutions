About TaxInfo.solutions

How we produce source-backed Cyprus tax explainers, maintain assumptions, and process corrections.

What This Site Is For

TaxInfo.solutions is an educational publishing project focused on Cyprus tax workflows for employees, payroll teams, founders, and investors. We publish practical guides and estimator tools that help readers move from policy documents to filing-ready checklists.

Content on this site is informational and does not replace advice from a licensed tax professional. When rules change, we update affected pages and provide revised review dates.

Editorial Standards

  • Primary sources first: We prioritize government publications, official forms, and public tax authority materials.
  • Clear assumptions: Calculator rates and scenario inputs are shown explicitly so users can verify or challenge them.
  • Plain language: We rewrite technical terms into actionable workflows with practical examples.
  • No auto-rewrite scraping: We do not copy bulk text from other sites.

Review and Update Policy

  • Scheduled checks: Core pages are reviewed monthly during filing season.
  • Event-based updates: We revise pages when thresholds, forms, or deadlines change.
  • Timestamping: Updated pages include a visible review date.
  • Version control: Changes are tracked and published through this repository.

How Calculator Content Is Built

Our calculator pages are not generic templates. We define explicit assumptions for each estimator (for example: income thresholds, social insurance caps, and scenario-specific deduction limits), then keep those assumptions aligned with published materials and update notes.

Input Design

  • Only fields that affect tax outcomes are included.
  • Optional fields include caps and validation hints.
  • Outputs are presented with line-by-line components.

Output Design

  • Users can compare years using the same assumptions.
  • Computation notes explain what is included and excluded.
  • Pages link back to source references and disclaimer text.

Corrections, Feedback, and Contact

If you find an error, outdated threshold, or unclear explanation, email us with the page URL and source reference so we can review quickly.

  • Corrections email: info@taxinfo.solutions
  • Response target: Within 5 business days for substantive corrections.
  • Commercial inquiries: info@taxinfo.solutions
