How to Read This Page

This changelog lists material updates that affect user interpretation or workflow decisions. Pure design tweaks and non-substantive formatting edits are excluded. Each entry includes the date, affected page, what changed, and why the change matters.

  • Correction: a factual or wording update that changes interpretation.
  • Expansion: additional practical detail added without changing core interpretation.
  • Integrity: link, metadata, or crawl-quality improvements.
